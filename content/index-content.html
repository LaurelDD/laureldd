Laurel is a design studio based in Costa Rica, dedicated
to creating thoughtfully crafted interiors and original furniture.
The studio thrives on close collaboration
— between our team, our clients, and our craftsmen —
to create spaces and pieces that go beyond trends
and enrich everyday experiences.

Interior Design

We work on a limited number of projects at a time, allowing María José our design director to remain intimately involved in the creative direction of each commission. Our interiors team—led by head of interior design Stephanie Arroyo—ensures meticulous execution from initial concept through to completion.

Our studio specializes in high-end residential projects and hospitality spaces. With a contemporary eclectic aesthetic, we embrace tropical materials, elaborate millwork and organic textures.

From a building's initial planning stages to 360° remodels, we manage projects at every scale. Every environment we design tells its own story, shaped by the unique lifestyle and personality of our clients. We welcome both international and local projects throughout Costa Rica and Central America.

Properties

Our property venture applies a decade of design expertise to select transformations. We acquire homes with exceptional bones and reimagine them completely—architecture, interiors, and furnishings—creating turn-key residences that embody sophisticated living with a laid-back approach.
