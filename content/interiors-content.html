Full-Scope Design Services

Creative partners from concept to completion.

Laurel Depto. de Diseño specializes in comprehensive interior design for upscale residential and hospitality projects throughout Costa Rica. With over a decade of experience, we've built a studio that exceeds the capabilities of a traditional design firm.

Our approach is holistic: we act as a valuable mediator between clients and construction teams, guiding projects from concept through completion. From luxury rental properties to high-end residences, our commitment to quality and attention to detail ensure that each space reflects our clients' needs and aspirations—whether large-scale renovations or thoughtfully curated interiors.

Virtual Consultations

Expert insight, flexible investment.

Leading your own project but seeking professional guidance? Our hourly virtual consultations offer flexible design support for clients who value expert insight without full-service engagement. These collaborative sessions are ideal for homeowners and property developers who need strategic direction—from space planning and material selection to color palettes and styling decisions.

As design director and founder, I personally lead each consultation, bringing our studio's decade of experience to your specific challenges and providing actionable design solutions tailored to your timeline and budget.
