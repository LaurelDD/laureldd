Our Process

Every step guided with expertise

1

Consultation

We meet with designers, clients, or project managers to discuss aesthetic preferences, functional needs, and specific requirements. Whether working from architectural plans or visiting an existing space, our team evaluates window orientation, dimensions, and room usage to recommend tailored solutions.

2

Design & Material Selection

We present fabric samples, hardware options, and design recommendations. From performance fabrics to luxury textiles, motorized systems to manual hardware — materials selected for beauty and durability. Detailed specifications ensure selections meet both aesthetic goals and practical requirements.

3

Custom Fabrication

Custom fabric treatments are crafted in our Costa Rican workshop to exact specifications — professional pleating, precision cutting, and meticulous finishing using techniques refined over three decades. We source motorized systems through our Asian product line and work in close collaboration with Lutron representatives for premium automation solutions. Rigorous quality checks ensure flawless construction throughout.

4

Professional Installation

Our team handles mounting, hanging, testing motorized systems, and final adjustments for perfect operation and appearance. We coordinate with your schedule for a seamless installation from start to finish.
